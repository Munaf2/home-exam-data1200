European Accessibility Act

Norwegian government chooses to implement the European Accessibility Act into Norwegian law and is prepared to issue large fines for digital services, or devices that do not comply and adapt to the basic principles of universal design.

Key terms

Universal design is based on creating products that ensure accessibility and usability for all people no matter their situation or state.

Important

Simple and Intuitive Use: The design should be easy to understand, regardless of the user's experience, knowledge, language skills, or current concentration level.

Smartphone Usage

Smartphones are widely used in the world and almost everyone has one. Most people rely on their smartphones to communicate with others and really depend on them to get through everyday life. of the following topics:

a) The smartphone is used to pay bills, or
b) Access personal files , and
c) Some people even rely on it to do their jobs.

Smartphones have many great built-in features that make it easier for people to use them such as screen readers, size adjustments and voice commands. This makes the technology more user friendly and more adaptable for everyone to use. Another way smartphones comply with universal design is that they get more simplified for every model put on the market.

New and smarter functions are added which makes the phone easier to use, such as voice and face recognition, read back and many customization options. Smartphones are also very accessible all over the world and come in a wide price range which makes it available for everyone no matter the income or economical situation.

First of all, more people will be able to use the products and they will be more user-friendly. This will make digital products more accessible and included to all people in society. If the product does not comply with the law, there will be economic consequences through fines. This could hurt small businesses who cannot afford to make a mistake in production and in the worst case could lead to bankruptcy.

Our assumption is that in the short-term digitalization will be slightly decreased since companies have to adapt and change their products, but in the long term more people will be able to use the products which will accelerate the technological digitalization. Also, smaller businesses could find it harder to get products approved since they do not have the money and competence to develop these types of products.
